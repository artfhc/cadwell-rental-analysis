AB 1482 — What You Need to Know

  • This ADU: Exempt from AB 1482 rent cap for 15 years (new construction through ~2041). Written exemption notice must be in the lease packet.
  • Main house on same lot: LOSES its single-family AB 1482 exemption once an ADU is added. Now subject to rent cap (CPI + 5%, max 10%; currently ~8.8% through July 31, 2026) and just-cause eviction rules.
  • Failure to serve exemption notice: $10,000+ liability risk.
  • Action: Consult a California landlord-tenant attorney before signing any lease.
